How AI attacks your infrastructure

Modern attacks use neural networks to probe, learn, and exploit your systems — automating what used to take a human hacker weeks.

🎯
AI reconnaissance
AI models scan your public footprint — ports, subdomains, tech stack, employee emails — in seconds, mapping attack surface automatically.
🧠
Vulnerability exploitation
Neural networks match discovered vulnerabilities to known exploits, auto-generating payload variants that evade signature-based detection.
🔄
Lateral movement & persistence
Once inside, AI-driven malware moves laterally, escalates privileges, and establishes persistence — all while mimicking legitimate user behaviour.
💀
Data exfiltration & ransom
Sensitive data extracted through encrypted channels, ransomware deployed, and double-extortion initiated — often before IT teams detect anything.

Our methodology

How a penetration test works

A structured, responsible approach modelled on real-world attacker methodology.

01 / RECON
Reconnaissance
Passive and active information gathering — OSINT, DNS enumeration, port scanning, and service fingerprinting.
02 / SCAN
Vulnerability scanning
Automated and manual scanning to identify CVEs, misconfigurations, and logic flaws across all in-scope systems.
03 / EXPLOIT
Exploitation
Controlled, safe exploitation of confirmed vulnerabilities to prove real-world impact without disrupting operations.
04 / PIVOT
Lateral movement
Testing how far an attacker could move once inside — privilege escalation, credential reuse, and internal pivoting.
05 / REPORT
Reporting & remediation
Full findings report with severity ratings, proof-of-concept evidence, and a prioritised remediation roadmap.
